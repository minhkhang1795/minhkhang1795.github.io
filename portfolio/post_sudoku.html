Robotic arm solves Sudoku

September - December 2017

This is one of my first projects in the Olin's Robotics Lab in the first semester of my freshman year. I joined the IRL team in the lab and the goal of the team is to control a robotic arm to interact with humans or to complete a task. I then teamed up with 2 other Oliners to work on the Sudoku project, which was quite challenging but very interesting to me because I love solving puzzles.

I was responsible for creating an algorithm to solve a Sudoku puzzle, finding a way to capture a sudoku board including recognizing and locating each digit on the board and actuating the robotic arm.

Check out the project on our Github repo.
